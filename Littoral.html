Welcome and discover how the people from the littoral conduct their custome wedding which is also known as traditional wedding

Here you will be able to discover how the littoral people conduct their weddings and usually how it differs from te bamilike and other people in Cameroon.

Names given to the people from the littoral regions

People from the littoral are genrally called the sawa people mostly the sawa ethnic group

Just like the bamilike, they also conduct their traditional wedding in the night For them its a way to call the ancestors to see what is happening, give their blessings and assist them

What and how it is done

First of all around 5pm in the night before te begining of the ceremonie, some tradional meals are cooked such as mbongo chobi which is a black soap Ndole, and mintouba so that those withnessing the ceremony should not be hungry . Also they produce natural drinks such as folere, baoba and share sugar in plastic to kids around the quater

The ceremonie usually starts around 7:30pm where we the participants sits and gather in a circular form around the compound. A speech is been said in a hierachy manner starting from the chief to the father ,uncles later bothers. The mothers are inside trrying to prepare the bride while the groom is sited outside with his family mebers

After the speech, the wedding ritual starts first of all the mothers will look for all ways of charging he groom family so that they can pay some extra -money especially if they came late

Before bringing the bride the mothers and aunty will complain and tell to the man that the aeroplane had a problem or the driver could not make it promptly that money is needed for the process
